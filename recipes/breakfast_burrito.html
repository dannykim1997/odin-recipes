Breakfast Burrito

These make ahead breakfast burritos are the perfect item to keep in your freezer

You can make several of these burritos at once and keep them in your freezer to reheat whenever you'd like.

Ingredients

  • Burrito Tortilla
  • Bacon
  • Hashbrown
  • Egg
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in pan and remove
  2. Cook hashbrown in pan and remove
  3. Cook eggs in pan and remove
  4. Heat up tortilla in pan and remove
  5. Add ingredients to tortilla and wrap into burrito
  6. optional Fry all sides on burrito to get crispy
