Breakfast Sandwich

These breakfast sandwiches are great to make ahead and keep in your freezer

You can make several of these sandwiches at once and keep them in your freezer to reheat whenever you'd like.

Ingredients

  • English Muffin
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in pan and remove
  2. Cook egg in pan and remove
  3. Split open english muffin
  4. Add bacon, egg, and slice of cheese to muffin
