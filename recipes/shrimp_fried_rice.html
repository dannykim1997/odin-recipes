Shrimp Fried Rice

This shrimp fried rice will taste better than takeout

Shrimp fried rice is one of the simplest delicious dishes you can cook at home.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Shrimp
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Green Onion
  • Frozen Peas & Carrots
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook your rice
  2. Pan fry shrimp
  3. Toss in peas and carrots
  4. Put in cooked rice
  5. Stir in egg
  6. Squirt soy sauce into fried rice
