Chicken Burrito Bowl

These chicken burrito bowls will hit your Chipotle fix at a fraction of the price

This is one of the easiest tasty high protein meals you can make at home. All it takes is a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Rice
  • Cabbage
  • Black Beans
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Frozen Corn

Steps

  1. Marinate and cook chicken thighs in your choice of marinade
  2. Cook rice
  3. Slice and cook cabbage
  4. Heat up black beans
  5. Heat up frozen corn
  6. Put all ingredients in bowl with shredded cheese
